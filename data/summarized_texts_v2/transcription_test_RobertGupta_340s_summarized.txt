Nathaniel Ayers was a Julliard trained double bassist whose promising career was cut short by a tragic affliction with paranoid schizophrenia. 30 years later he was living homeless on the streets of Skid Row in downtown Los Angeles. Ayers'' manic rage was transformed into understanding, into understanding and acquired curiosity and grace. Music is an escape from his tormented state, and I understood that this was the very essence of art. This is the very reason why we made music: we take something that exists within all of us, through our creativity, we're able to shape those emotions into reality.
