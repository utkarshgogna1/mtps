"I was a real science geek, but it was all about trying to understand the world, understand the limits of possibility" "My love of science fiction seemed to be mirrored in the world around me" "I've learned that deep ocean environment and even the shallow oceans are rich with amazing life" In the mid-90s he started a company with Stan Winston-my good friend Stan Winston, who was the premier makeup and creature designer at that time- and it was called Digital Domain. The company would leapfrog, pass the analog processes of optical printers and on, and we would go right to digital production. But we found ourselves lagging in the creature and character design stuff that we had founded the company to do. "I was completely smitten by this and had to do more, and I made a curious decision. I said, OK, I'm going to park my day job as a Hollywood moviemaker, and Iam going to go be a full-time explorer" The film was made for a very limited audience, no fame, no glory, no money. What are you doing? You're doing it for the task itself, For the challenge- and the ocean is the most challenging environment there is. The respect of your team is more important than all the laurels in the world.
